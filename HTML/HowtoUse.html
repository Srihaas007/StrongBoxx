To use the Strongboxx system, follow these steps:




    1. Initiate the transaction: As a buyer or seller, initiate a transaction and agree on the terms and conditions with the other party.

    2. Transfer funds: Transfer the agreed-upon funds to the Strongboxx account, which will hold them in escrow until the transaction is completed.

    3. Confirm transaction: Once the transaction is completed, confirm the receipt of the goods or services to release the funds to the seller.

    4. Dispute resolution: If there is a dispute, contact the Strongboxx team, who will investigate based on the agreed-upon terms and conditions and decide whether to release the funds to the seller or refund them to the customer.
